◆ displayNumberOnTrackerLeds()

void lib::displayNumberOnTrackerLeds ( uint8_t  number)

Use the line tracker LEDs to display a number (from 1 to 5). Providing 0 as a number will turn all tracker LEDs off. number The number to display with the LEDs (indexed from 1 and viewed from left to right when looking at the robot from behind). Values above 5 will be clamped to 5, and zero will turn off LEDs.
